Description

Abstract / Description:
This dataset contains the relief layer of Queensland. It is a version derived from the DNRM regional mapping series which depicts road classification, markers and distances, railways, drainage, populated centres, coast, reef and relief. See additional information also.
Purpose:
The data can be integrated with other General Purpose Map data over Queensland to serve business and social or planning needs. The data is suitable only for broad scale use.

This dataset contains the relief layer of Queensland. It is a version derived from the DNRM regional mapping series whichdepicts road classification, markers and distances, railways, drainage, populated centres, coast, reef and relief. This dataset should be used in conjunction with the other datasets in the series which when combined form a Queensland reference map. The datasets are image background, major drainage layer, map major place names layer, major railway layer, major road network and outline layer.

History:

Lineage statement:
A 'General Purpose Map' produced by the former department of Mapping and Surveying (published in 1978) was used to create the theme and content of this datasets. In 2003, film positives of the original hand scribed map overlays were scan digitised. Base data was formed to a precision of 0.2mm centreline to centreline. Text was captured by key entry, updates were derived from the Digital Cadastral Database (DCDB) extraction, other departmental regional map sources (currently at 2006 version) were also used. Data from the original hand scribe maps was updated / and or replaced in subsequent versions to produce the current datasets at a publication scale of 1:2,500,000.
